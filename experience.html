Experience/Leadership

Jobs, Clubs, and Service

Posted by Katelyn Kellis on April 24, 2024

Jobs

 

Freedom Prep Academy

I was a teacher aide and summer school counselor for Freedom Prep Academy from July 2022 until August 2023. I taught and tutored children between the ages of 5 and 9 in math and ELA. I also created lesson plans centered around teamwork and collaboration.

Bluejay Creative Media

I was a social media manager for Bluejay Creative Media from March 2021 until June 2022. During this time, I created social media ads and researched material for relevant ads.

Clubs/Service

 

United Sound

United Sound is a school club that allowed me to teach violin to high school students with disabilities. I taught them how to read music and learn pieces. I assisted these students in performances where they were able to showcase what they learned.

Latino Advocacy Club

I participated in Latino Advocacy Club for several years. I planned, set up, and ran festivals designed around celebrating Latino culture. We donated the money raised at these festivals to various charities.

Model UN

In Model UN, I represented the Greek Economic and Social Council. I had to write speeches and plan ways to fix various social and economic issues in Greece. I met with other representatives to debate the best ways to solve these issues.
